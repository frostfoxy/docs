## **Dependency**

**Metamask**:

- Download Metamask from the **[Metamask Website](https://metamask.io/download.htm).**
- Once installed, follow the instructions to import an existing wallet or create a new one.

## Staking Guide

### 1. Connect Wallet

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/) and click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your Metamask wallet.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_1.png)

### 2. Select Network

Click on the Network button and select BSC Chain. A Metamask prompt will appear to switch your metamask network. Click on “Switch network” to continue.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_2.png)

### 3. Visit rBNB Dashboard

Click the "Stake" button to access the [rBNB Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/BNB?net=BEP20).

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_3.png)

### 4. I**nput staking amount**

Enter the amount of BNB you would like to stake. Review the amount of rBNB you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

Note: The minimum staking amount required is XXX BNB.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_4.png)

### 5. **Confirm transaction and await for confirmations**

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_5.png)

### 6. **Add rETH to your wallet**

Navigate to the [rBNB Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/BNB?net=BEP20) and click on the "Add rBNB to Wallet" button. A Metamask prompt will appear, select "Add token" to include rBNB in your wallet.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_6.png)

### 7. **Track Rewards**

Monitor your staked BNB and its rewards by visiting your **[Profile](https://test-app.stafi.io/profile/evm?net=BEP20)**.

![BNB Staking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_7.png)

## Unstaking Guide

### 1. **Visit rBNB Dashboard**

Visit the [rBNB Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/BNB?net=BEP20), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![BNB Unstaking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_8.png)

### 2. **Input unstaking amount**

Enter the desired amount of rBNB you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![BNB Unstaking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_9.png)

### 3. **Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations**

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the spending cap of BNB and approve it.

Once confirmed, a second transaction pop-up for staking will appear. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

*Note: Unbonding rBNB takes 16 days before it can be withdrawn.*

![BNB Unstaking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_10.png)

### 4. **Withdraw unstaked tokens**

rBNB will need to be manually withdrawn from the Withdraw Tab on the rBNB Dashboard once the unbonding period has ended. Click on “Withdraw” button to proceed.

![BNB Unstaking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_11.png)

### 5. **Receive tokens**

Verify the transaction and the associated Gas fees on your Metamask Wallet. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Upon confirmation of the transaction, BNB tokens will be withdrawn and sent to your wallet.

![BNB Unstaking](/image/stakingunstakingevmbnb_12.png)
